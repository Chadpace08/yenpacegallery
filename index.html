Know Your Artist

The Story
of Yen Pace

Yen is a Rizal-based artist born in Makati and raised across the vibrant regions of Davao and Cebu. A dedicated mother of three, an entrepreneur, a certified public accountant — and now, answering her calling as a freelance artist. Her family roots run deep in architecture and fine arts.

Her artistic journey began in early childhood, honing her skills through local and international painting contests, earning recognition from government and private sponsors — notably SM, Ayala, and a feature in SunStar Cebu. Art was never merely a hobby; it funded her education as an academic scholar who also worked part-time.

Yen's abstract journey began serendipitously during the pandemic, when a buyer chose a background painting from her online goods. This chance encounter led to selling hundreds of textured abstract paintings — locally and internationally — from 2021 to 2025.

Her art is deeply rooted in faith, drawing inspiration from God's creation: soil, water, caves, clouds, and wood. Each piece a rich, layered interpretation of the world around us. Beyond the canvas, Yen uses her art for good — donating proceeds to calamity victims and children in need.
